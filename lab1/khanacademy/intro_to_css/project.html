Travel to Switzerland

Switzerland is a small and cozy country in the very center of Europe. Due to its multinationality and linguistic diversity, it seems huge. Here you will find a rare combination of natural and man-made attractions. The visiting card of Switzerland is the majestic mountains covered with sparkling snow, picturesque valleys, crystal clear lakes and fast mountain rivers. Switzerland is order and peace, elegant cities and romantic villages nestled in picturesque valleys between gorges. All this causes sincere delight among tourists.


Geographically, the country consists of 4 parts:

  • Swiss Alps
  • Central Switzerland
  • Fribourg
  • Lake Geneva region

Swiss Alps

The Swiss Alps are a mountainous region that is home to some of the best ski resorts in the world, attracting millions of winter sports enthusiasts every year. There are thousands of kilometers of slopes of varying difficulty, pristine freeride slopes, snowboard parks and many other activities. Those who are tired of skiing can spend time at thermal springs, spas and interesting festivals. In the Swiss Alps, there are several of the highest mountain objects at once - a revolving restaurant, a metro and a railway station.

Central Switzerland

The central part of Switzerland consists of four forested cantons with a unique culture. Vast landscapes, shimmering waters and towering mountain peaks of the Alps make this place a stunning source of vitality for all visitors. This region is worth visiting for the beauty of Lake Lucerne surrounded by the highest mountains. Here, time slows down, and untouched nature gives a new breath of energy. The resort town of Engelberg is ideal for hiking and winter holidays. The village of Einsiedeln is famous for its male Benedictine monastery, which houses a unique figure of the Madonna in black.

Lake Geneva region

The amazing and incredibly picturesque Leman region seems to have all the attributes you need for a luxurious and memorable vacation. Despite the external gloss, a large number of luxury hotels and yachts, local resorts are distinguished by their extraordinary hospitality and pleasant atmosphere. Instead of pathos and pomposity, the guests of the region are waiting for a hospitable welcome and a lot of unforgettable impressions. It is worth coming here for the sake of unique natural treasures and cinematic landscapes, the cleanest air and famous vineyards, ancient architecture and unique local flavor. The Leman region will find something to surprise the traveler, giving everyone vivid emotions and warm memories.
